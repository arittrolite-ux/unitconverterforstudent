⚙️ Kinematics

Speed

Distance travelled per unit time.

v = d / t
Unit: m/s

Velocity

Displacement per unit time.

v = displacement / time
Unit: m/s

Acceleration

Rate of change of velocity.

a = (v-u)/t
Unit: m/s²

First Equation of Motion

v = u + at

Second Equation of Motion

s = ut + ½at²

Third Equation of Motion

v² = u² + 2as

🏋️ Dynamics

Force

F = ma
Unit: Newton (N)

Momentum

p = mv
Unit: kg·m/s

Impulse

J = Ft
Unit: N·s

Friction

F = μN
Unit: N

⚡ Work, Energy & Power

Work Done

Work is done when a force moves an object.

W = F × s
Unit: Joule (J)

Power

Rate of doing work.

P = W / t
Unit: Watt (W)

Kinetic Energy

Energy due to motion.

KE = ½mv²
Unit: Joule (J)

Potential Energy

Energy due to position.

PE = mgh
Unit: Joule (J)

Mechanical Energy

ME = KE + PE
Unit: Joule (J)

Efficiency

η = (Useful Output/Input) × 100%
Unit: %
