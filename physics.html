⚙️ Kinematics

Speed

Distance travelled per unit time.

v = d / t
Unit: m/s

Velocity

Displacement per unit time.

v = displacement / time
Unit: m/s

Acceleration

Rate of change of velocity.

a = (v-u)/t
Unit: m/s²

First Equation of Motion

v = u + at

Second Equation of Motion

s = ut + ½at²

Third Equation of Motion

v² = u² + 2as

🏋️ Dynamics

Force

F = ma
Unit: Newton (N)

Momentum

p = mv
Unit: kg·m/s

Impulse

J = Ft
Unit: N·s

Friction

F = μN
Unit: N

⚡ Work, Energy & Power

Work Done

Work is done when a force moves an object.

W = F × s
Unit: Joule (J)

Power

Rate of doing work.

P = W / t
Unit: Watt (W)

Kinetic Energy

Energy due to motion.

KE = ½mv²
Unit: Joule (J)

Potential Energy

Energy due to position.

PE = mgh
Unit: Joule (J)

Mechanical Energy

ME = KE + PE
Unit: Joule (J)

Efficiency

η = (Useful Output/Input) × 100%
Unit: %

🌍 Gravitation

Newton's Law of Gravitation

F = Gm₁m₂/r²
Unit: Newton (N)

Weight

W = mg
Unit: Newton (N)

Gravitational Potential Energy

PE = mgh
Unit: Joule (J)

Escape Velocity

Ve = √(2GM/R)
Unit: m/s

Orbital Velocity

Vo = √(GM/R)
Unit: m/s

🔥 Heat & Thermodynamics

Heat Energy

Q = mcΔT
Unit: Joule (J)

Latent Heat

Q = mL
Unit: Joule (J)

Specific Heat Capacity

c = Q/(mΔT)
Unit: J/kg°C

Thermal Expansion

ΔL = αLΔT

Ideal Gas Law

PV = nRT

🌊 Waves & Sound

Wave Speed

v = fλ
Unit: m/s

Frequency

f = 1/T
Unit: Hz

Time Period

T = 1/f
Unit: s

Angular Frequency

ω = 2πf
Unit: rad/s

Sound Intensity

I = P/A
Unit: W/m²

Echo Distance

d = vt/2
Unit: m

Doppler Effect

f' = f(v±vo)/(v∓vs)

Wave Number

k = 2π/λ

💡 Light & Optics

Mirror Formula

1/f = 1/u + 1/v

Magnification

m = v/u

Lens Formula

1/f = 1/v - 1/u

Power of Lens

P = 1/f
Unit: Diopter

Snell's Law

n₁sinθ₁ = n₂sinθ₂

Refractive Index

n = c/v

Critical Angle

sin C = 1/n

Young's Double Slit

β = λD/d
